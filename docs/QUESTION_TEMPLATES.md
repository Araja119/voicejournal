# VoiceJournal - Question Templates Database

## Overview

These are the pre-made questions the app suggests based on the relationship type. Users can select from these or create their own custom questions.

---

## Parent Questions

### Childhood & Growing Up
1. What's your earliest memory?
2. What was your favorite thing to do as a child?
3. What was your childhood home like?
4. Who was your best friend growing up, and what did you do together?
5. What was school like for you?
6. What did you want to be when you grew up?
7. What was the best gift you ever received as a child?
8. What family traditions did you have growing up?
9. What was your favorite meal your parents made?
10. What got you in trouble as a kid?

### Family History
11. Tell me about your parents. What were they like?
12. What do you know about your grandparents?
13. Do you know how your parents met?
14. What values did your parents teach you?
15. What's a story about our family that should never be forgotten?
16. Is there anything about our family history you wish you knew more about?

### Life Lessons & Wisdom
17. What's the best advice you've ever received?
18. What do you wish you had known at 20?
19. What are you most proud of in your life?
20. What's a mistake you learned the most from?
21. If you could go back and change one decision, would you? What would it be?
22. What does a good life mean to you?

### Relationships & Love
23. How did you meet mom/dad?
24. What made you fall in love with them?
25. What's the secret to a lasting relationship?
26. What was your wedding day like?
27. When did you know they were "the one"?

### Parenting & Family
28. What was it like when you found out you were going to be a parent?
29. What do you remember about the day I was born?
30. What's your favorite memory of me as a child?
31. What was the hardest part of raising kids?
32. What's something you hope I always remember?
33. Is there anything you wish you had done differently as a parent?
34. What are your hopes and dreams for me?

### Career & Accomplishments
35. What jobs have you had throughout your life?
36. What was your dream job?
37. What accomplishment are you most proud of?
38. Who influenced your career the most?
39. What would you do differently in your career if you could start over?

### Fun & Personal
40. What's the happiest day of your life?
41. What's a trip or adventure you'll never forget?
42. What music defined your younger years?
43. What's your favorite book or movie and why?
44. If you could have dinner with anyone from history, who would it be?
45. What's something most people don't know about you?

## Implementation Notes

1. **Display Logic:** When user adds a person with relationship type "parent," show Parent Questions first, sorted by category

2. **Randomize Option:** Offer "surprise me" button that picks 5-10 random questions from the relevant set

3. **Starter Packs:** Offer curated sets like:
   - "Quick Start" (5 essential questions)
   - "Life Story" (20 comprehensive questions)
   - "Just for Fun" (10 light-hearted questions)

4. **Custom Questions:** Always allow users to write their own, stored with source="custom"

5. **Premium Upsell:** Free users get access to all template questions but limited custom questions (e.g., 5 custom per journal on free tier)
